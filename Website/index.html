Introducing Alexa Go

Core Values

The Inspiration behind Alexa Go

Mobility

Traditional home assistants are static and gimmicky. We want a home companion that can physically do something.

Compatibility

With the universality of voice commands, we want to create a companion device that can aid the lives of everyone.

Adaptability

As machine intelligence grows, we want to incorporate its power to create a helper that learns to improve.

Not just a smart home

Through the integrated Amazon Echo technology, Alexa Go can recognize your voice and act based on your commands. With its tracks, it can accompany you anywhere, making it a true smart companion.

Accomadation: Personalized

Voice commands allow Alexa Go to work with a wide variety of people, ranging from children, to the visually impaired, to the elderly.

Growing with You.

Alexa Go can adapt and learn to accommodate your specific requests, resulting in an efficient partner in life. Over time, it can track requests to aid in the most effective way possible.

Alexa Go: Mark I

Alexa Go Mark I currently features 3 point tracks for mobility across multiple surfaces. It includes a robotic arm with grip sensors to ensure the best grip possible for any object. Currently, you can send Alexa Go to retrieve objects for you, in addition to all the functions the standard Alexa brings to the table. Additionally, Alexa Go can aid in times of crises by providing a calming presence, making it a perfect addition to ever-changing households.
